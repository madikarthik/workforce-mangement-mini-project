This program helps you to understand how the your employees are working
1. company workforce details
	by pressing 1 in the menu it will display the complete list of employees working in
	your company
2. P.A.A.M analysis
	by pressing 2 in the menu it will display the totel analysis of the workforce
3.Add a team
	by pressing 3 you can add a team/department.
4. Delete Team
	by pressing 4 you can delete a team/department.
5. Search for an employee
	by pressing 5 you can search any employee in the company by there ID. it will show
	you the details of the employee like name of the team,Credits assigned to him.
7. logout 
	by pressing 7 you can logout of you account and go back to main menu
